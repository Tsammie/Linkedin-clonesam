About

I'm someone who takes real pride in growth; both in where I've come from and where I'm heading.

I started out in Aerospace Engineering, where I learned how to think critically, solve complex problems, and stay curious. That foundation has shaped the way I work today as a Data Analyst.

Now, I spend my time making sense of data: cleaning it, analyzing it, and turning it into insights people can actually use. I've built reporting tools that save hours of work, visualized performance trends for schools, and helped teams make faster, smarter decisions.

The part I'm most proud of? I've been learning as I go; and growing fast. Each project gives me more confidence, and each challenge pushes me closer to where I want to be: a future in data science and machine learning.

This is just the beginning, and I'm excited for what's next. Feel free to connect or reach out if you're into data, tech, or just love solving problems.
